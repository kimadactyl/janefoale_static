Curriculum Vitae – Jane Foale

Qualifications and Education

  • BA Fine Art, Leeds College of Art, 2010
  • HE Certificate in Contemporary Art Practice, University of Leeds, 2005
  • PG Diploma in Business Administration, University of Bradford, 1996
  • BA Hons. Sociology, Durham University, 1973

Courses and personal research have been undertaken at the University of Leeds’ Departments of Earth Sciences and Colour Chemistry, and at the Colour Museum in Bradford.

Residencies and other Projects

  • Collaborative project in the Coniston Copper Mines Valley, Cumbria with the Ruskin Museum and Cumbrian Amenity Trust Mining History Society. April 2011 – November 2012.
  • Visiting Artist at Killhope - the North of England Lead Mining Museum, Co. Durham. May 2009 – May 2012.
  • The minerals and landscape of the Caldbeck Fells, Cumbria. January – December 2009.
  • Artist in Residence, Killhope – the North of England Lead Mining Museum, Co. Durham. May 2008 – May 2009.
  • Residency, Cliffe Castle Museum, Keighley, W. Yorkshire. October 2006 – May 2008.
